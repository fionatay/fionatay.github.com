I'm currently a Rails developer at Pivotal Labs in San Francisco.

I earned my B.S. in Computer Science at Harvey Mudd College, where I became a CS major because of the department's initiatives to make CS more welcoming.

Because of my experience at Mudd, I'm passionate about getting more women into CS. I recently ran a conference workshop at Grace Hopper 2012 on how to start women in CS activities at your organizations. I also run a support group for students running women in CS activities.

Lately I've been contributing to a registry for stolen bikes and an educational nonprofit. In the past I've also contributed to halloween helper, a satire of Halloween sexualization. I also like to crochet, bike and play soccer.
